Catch Up on Recent Events

Here's a look at some of the impactful events we've hosted over the past year. These events have brought together the community, raised awareness, and driven meaningful change for African children and adolescents.

Back-to-School Drive

Date: September 15, 2024

Description: This event focused on equipping children from underserved communities with school supplies for the new academic year. Volunteers distributed backpacks, books, and stationery to ensure students are well-prepared for their studies.

Health and Wellness Fair

Date: August 5, 2024

Description: A day dedicated to promoting physical and mental health among children and their families. The fair featured free health screenings, nutritional counseling, fitness workshops, and mental health resources, helping hundreds of attendees access essential services.

Youth Empowerment Workshop

Date: July 10, 2024

Description: A workshop aimed at empowering adolescents with leadership, communication, and entrepreneurial skills. The workshop featured talks from successful young entrepreneurs and interactive sessions designed to foster confidence and creativity.

Community Cleanup and Green Initiative

Date: June 22, 2024

Description: Volunteers came together to clean local parks and plant trees as part of an environmental conservation initiative. The event highlighted the importance of protecting the environment and fostering a sense of responsibility among young participants.

Art for Change Exhibition

Date: May 18, 2024

Description: A powerful art exhibition showcasing work from young artists. The theme of the exhibition centered on social change, with each piece reflecting the dreams and struggles of African youth. Funds raised from the exhibition supported arts education programs.

International Children's Day Celebration

Date: April 2, 2024

Description: A fun-filled day celebrating children from all walks of life. The event included games, performances, and educational workshops aimed at fostering creativity and collaboration among children while raising awareness about their rights and well-being.
